Welcome to Rogers CBU Analytics Insights Portal (the “site”). If you’ve come this far you’re looking to tap into the data and insights prepared by the CBU Team on Viewership Analytics. Acceptable use of the data and insights available here requires acceptance of the following terms in addition to Rogers standard information security and data privacy terms:
  • Data will not be combined with non-Residential subscriber (e.g. Shopping Channel, other Rogers entities)
  • If data is to be shared with other teams, you need to refresh your access, your role changes or others on your team require access, please contact the Site Administrator
  • Login credentials are unique to you and should not be shared. If additional users require access please direct them to the Site Administrator
  • You will not use this site or the analytics applications linked from this site for any malicious activity
  • You will not solicit or sell Rogers customer or business information for unlawful purposes

Rogers will use reasonable efforts to ensure that this site and the analytics applications are available twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week. However, there will be occasions when the services will be interrupted for maintenance, upgrades, data refreshes and emergency repairs or due to failures of telecommunications links and equipment. By clicking “I accept” you agree to responsible usage of the data and insights available through this analytics application.

If you have any questions, please direct them to the Site Administrator (Yanna Lai - yanna.lai@rci.rogers.com) or Sabrina Lising (sabrina.lising@rci.rogers.com).
